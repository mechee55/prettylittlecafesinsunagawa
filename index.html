Pretty Little Cafés

in Sunagawa

Good communication is just as stimulating as black coffee,

and just as hard to sleep after.

- Anne Morrow Lindbergh

When I think of Sunagawa, I think of those perfect summer nights during the colorful night market week. That one late afternoon in the autumn sun with my sister in front of the house and the smell of our dinner being cooked inside it. That sorbet-like snow covered with soot on the streets at the end of the winter. Then the spring's greenest green I have not seen anywhere else since I left the town 17 years ago.

This town holds a lot of dear and bitter memories for me. Nothing really changes there, but I've decided to rediscover it the way I like; cafés.
